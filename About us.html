About Us

About Us:

Welcome to Apple Service Center, your trusted destination for all Apple product repairs and services. As an Apple Authorized Service Provider, we are committed to delivering the highest quality service and support for your Apple devices.

Our Services:

Certified Repairs:

Our Apple-certified technicians use only genuine Apple parts to ensure your device functions as intended. Whether it’s an iPhone, iPad, Mac, or any other Apple product, we handle it all with precision and care.

Battery Replacement:

We offer battery replacement services for all Apple devices. If your device’s battery holds less than 80% of its original capacity and you have AppleCare+, the replacement is at no additional cost.

Screen Repair:

Cracked screen? No problem. We provide screen repair services covered by AppleCare+ for accidental damage.

Warranty Services:

We honor the Apple Limited Warranty and AppleCare+ plans, ensuring your device is covered for manufacturing issues and accidental damage.
